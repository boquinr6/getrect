Intro

In Shibu's Sushi Shop, there's a pesky little mouse trying to feed himself with Shibu's Sushi. Play as the mousey or play as chef Shibu!

Goals

  • The chef’s goal is to crush or trap the mouse with sushi rolls.
  • The mouse’s goal is to get to the goal by climbing sushi rolls that are dropped by the chef.

How to Play

Chef

  1. Before you drop rolls, you get to choose among three different rolls to drop.
  2. After you choose your roll, use (WASD) to move the roll as it falls. Try to trap or crush the moouse with the rolls you pick.

Mouse

  • Use (LEFT, RIGHT) to move and (UP) to jump.
  • Avoid rolls and try to jump on them to get to higher ground and reach the goal.
  • Mouse has the ability to accelerate every 2 seconds by pressing (SPACEBAR)

Game End

The game ends when either the mouse’s winning condition or losing condition is met.

Winning Condition

The mouse reaches the goal and wins! The chef loses.

Losing Condition

The chef successfully traps or crushes the mouse. Chef wins.
